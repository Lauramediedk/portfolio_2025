BikeMate

BikeMate is an innovative social biking application, created from a spark of inspiration during my internship at Bikekey. The challenge of encouraging more people to cycle piqued my interest, leading me to develop BikeMate for my final project at KEA. Utilizing technologies like Flask, Alpine.js, and HTMX, I created a platform that's both robust and user-friendly. The product is based on research I gathered through surveys which included questions about social media patterns, motivational drivers, and user habits. This data-driven approach has been effective in tailoring the web app's design to truly resonate with its target audience. Before developing the webapp I made sure to organise the requirements with a requirements specification, and structured my work in trello, with an approach similar to kanban.

BikeMate aims to transform cycling into a communal activity. It connects users, encouraging more frequent cycling through features like event creation and a friend system that keeps users informed about their friends cycling activities.
