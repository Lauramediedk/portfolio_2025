Echelon salon is a hair salon located in Copenhagen. They were in need of a website in order to be able to stand out, and attract more attention. Through the whole process I worked closely together with the owner of echelon, and we decided on a calm and warm look for the website, which should match the feeling of the salon. The website is built from scratch in HTML, CSS and Javascript and is one of my earlier projects, and here I learned a lot from the process as I also worked with a SEO expert. He enlightened me about his methods, and gave me an insight into the importance of SEO.
