Goal directed design

During my time at KEA we build a prototype for a digital meetings platform where the goal of the whole process was to become familiar with the concept of goal directed design. We therefore went through phases such as research, modeling, requirements definition, framework definiton etc... In these phases we learned about idea generating methods such as the design charette and various types of brainstorms. We also learned about terms such as context scenarios in order to gain a better understanding of how the user would interact with the product and therefore lead us to a better understanding of the core problem and the user's needs. We also made sure to divide our requirements into key-path, necessary, alternative and edge case scenarios, in order to adress all aspects of the user experience. Lastly we ended up with a detailed styletile which shows design choices for the user interface, and works like a guide.
