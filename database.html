Database

For an exam project we were to design and create a database. Here I first created an entity diagram over the different tables and their relations, as I were creating a relational database, and wanted to follow the rules of normalization in order to avoid redundant data, and make the process of updating the data easier. During the project I became very fond of SQL and gained knowledge about joins, Full-text search and ACID principles etc. I became familiar with terms such as primary key, foreign key, compound and composite keys and learned about the different types of tables such as lookup and junction etc. Lastly I also looked at other noSQL databases, where I experimented with creating document databases in collections of JSON, and made more complex relations in surrealDB, a graph database.
